What do I do??


Hello! I'm Radhika, a passionate researcher and a student of the intricate world of algorithms and graph theory, currently pursuing a Master by Research at IIIT Bangalore. My academic journey also includes delving into data science through a degree in Data Science and Applications from IIT Madras. My research focuses on developing, optimizing and understanding efficient algorithms that can solve complex problems in real world.

Beyond the realms of academia, I have a profound love for creativity. Whether it's doodling sketches that capture the essence of my thoughts or crafting digital art through graphic designing, creativity is a significant part of my life. This blend of analytical rigor and artistic expression defines who I am and drives my dual pursuit of innovation in technology and art.

Through my work, I aspire to contribute meaningful insights to the field of algorithms and data science, while also embracing and sharing my artistic endeavors. Thank you for visiting my site, and I look forward to connecting with fellow researchers and creatives alike.

P.S: If you are interested in a deeper dive into my research areas or would like to see my latest work in graph theory and data science, please visit my blog. I regularly share updates, insights, and discussions that illuminate the complexities and fascinations of my academic and creative pursuits. Join me in exploring the ever-evolving world of algorithms and design!!
